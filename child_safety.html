Privacy Policy – Anniversary Birthday Reminder

Effective Date: 06 April 2025

Anniversary Birthday Reminder (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

🧒 Age Requirement

Our app is designed for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we learn that a child under 13 has provided us with information, we will delete it immediately.

📆 What Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Names and dates you enter for birthdays and anniversaries (stored locally on your device)
  • Optional settings or preferences within the app

We do not collect personal contact details, addresses, or email addresses.

🔐 How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Remind you about birthdays and anniversaries
  • Customize your app experience
  • Show relevant ads (only if ads are enabled in the app)

📡 Third-Party Services

Our app may use trusted third-party services such as:

  • Analytics (e.g., Google Firebase) to understand how the app is used

These services may collect non-personal information such as device type, usage patterns, and general location (not exact address).

We only use Google-certified ad networks for apps directed to children and teens.

🔒 Data Security

All data you enter is either stored locally on your device or protected through trusted third-party services. We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

📝 Your Choices

You can:

  • Delete any birthdays or anniversaries you add
  • Uninstall the app at any time to remove your data
